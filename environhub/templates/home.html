Our Purpose

The purpose of this website is to increase online awareness about ecological issues pertaining to India, thereby hoping to develop pressure on the concerned authorities to solve these issues faster an more efficiently.




Choose

View our map of India where we have categorized each issue geographically and also indicated the intensity of online awareness of each issue.

Read

Our collection of hand-picked stories from the farthest corners of India, that we collect from the people who were most impacted by the issue.


Analyze

View specific events in the timeline of each of these events, analyze our statistics and try to correlate them and see how concerned various people in the country are.

Discuss

After you are done reading, take some time off and discuss on the same with our other readers, because as you know, knowledge grows by sharing.
